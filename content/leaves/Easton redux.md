title: NJ Lawsuit

In 1832, Lunaapeew in Wisconsin successfully petition the State of New Jersey for compensation based on claims to lost fishing and hunting rights promised by the Treaty of Easton. Bartholomew Calvin, who had moved to Wisconsin in 1823, serves as attorney for Lunaapeew.
